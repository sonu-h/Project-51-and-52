Fun Facts:

* The fastest time to make 100 origami cranes is 40 minutes 35 seconds
* The paper crane is a symbol of peace, love, and hope, and healing during challenging times
* The origami crane originated in Japan, with some roots going back to China

More Fun Facts:

* Origami is derived from two Japanese words: Ori (folded) and Kami (paper).
* No scissors allowed!
* Traditional origami uses a piece of paper in the shape of a square.
